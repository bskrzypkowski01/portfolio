### 🏆 [IFEC 2025](./IFEC%202025)
Winner of the International Future Energy Challenge (IFEC) 2025 — a prestigious global competition for students organized under the auspices of IEEE, the world's largest organization of technical professionals, focused on innovations in power electronics.

This year's challenge was to design and build a high-efficiency isolated DC-DC converter suitable for AI data center power supply applications.

Our team passed the selection stage, competed in the semifinals at APEC conference in Atlanta, USA (March 2025), advanced to the final held in Taiwan (July 2025), and was named the best team in the world among the top 8 finalists.
